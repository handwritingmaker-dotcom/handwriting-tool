## What Makes a Text to Handwriting Tool Realistic?

Realism is not one setting. It is a combination of small choices.

### Choose a Readable Handwriting Style

Decorative cursive fonts may look pretty, but they often look unrealistic for assignments or notes. A natural student-style font is usually better.

Our guide to the [best handwriting fonts for assignments](/blog/best-handwriting-fonts-for-assignments) explains which font styles work best for academic pages.

### Use Natural Spacing

If letters are too close, the page looks crowded. If they are too far apart, it looks artificial. Good tools let you adjust line spacing, word spacing, and margins.

### Pick the Right Paper Style

Lined paper is best for assignments and school notes. Blank paper is better for letters or clean printable documents. Graph paper can work for math or science-style notes.

### Export in the Right Format

Use PDF when you need a complete printable document. Use PNG or JPG when you need an image. The best tools support more than one format so you do not need a separate converter afterward.
