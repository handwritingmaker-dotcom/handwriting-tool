## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Making Text Too Small

Tiny handwriting may fit more words, but it becomes hard to read. Use a natural size.

### Using Fancy Fonts

Fancy cursive may look attractive, but it can feel fake for assignments. Choose a readable style.

### Ignoring Margins

Margins make handwritten pages look organized. Without margins, the page can look crowded.

### Downloading Before Previewing

Always preview before export. A few spacing changes can make the PDF look much better.
