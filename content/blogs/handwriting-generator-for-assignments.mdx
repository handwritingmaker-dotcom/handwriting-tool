## Real vs Fake Handwriting Output

Not all generated handwriting looks real. The difference between realistic and artificial output comes down to a few important factors.

| Factor | Unrealistic Output | Realistic Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Spacing | Perfect and uniform | Slightly uneven |
| Font | Decorative or fancy | Simple and natural |
| Alignment | Completely straight | Slight variation |
| Paragraphs | Long blocks | Short and natural |

If your output looks too perfect, it usually appears artificial. Small imperfections actually make handwriting look more natural.

## Best Settings for Realistic Handwritten Assignments

This is the most important part of the entire process. Most people ignore these settings, and that is why their output looks fake.

### Font Size

Use a medium size. Too small looks compressed and too large looks unnatural.

### Line Spacing

Keep spacing natural. Perfect spacing can make the text look computer-generated.

### Margins

Add proper margins similar to notebook pages. Keep the left margin slightly wider.

### Ink Color

Use blue or black. These are standard for assignments.

### Paragraph Length

Avoid writing long paragraphs. Break content into smaller sections.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many users make simple mistakes that ruin the final result.

- Using fancy fonts that do not look natural
- Keeping spacing too perfect
- Writing very long paragraphs
- Ignoring margins and alignment
- Choosing unrealistic colors

Avoiding these mistakes can instantly improve your output quality.
