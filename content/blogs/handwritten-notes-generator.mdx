## How to Structure Notes Before Converting

The best handwritten-style notes start with clean source text. Before you paste content into the converter, reduce the page to what you actually need for revision.

Use this simple structure:

1. Topic heading
2. Three to five key points
3. One short example
4. Important definitions or formulas
5. Quick recap at the end

This structure works better than pasting long textbook paragraphs. It creates printable notes that look organized and are easier to revise later.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

Avoid using too much text on one page. Avoid decorative handwriting for serious study notes. Do not ignore margins. Do not skip previewing. Do not export before checking whether the notes are readable. Also avoid weak ink colors that may look nice on screen but print poorly.

The best handwritten notes are not the fanciest. They are the clearest.
