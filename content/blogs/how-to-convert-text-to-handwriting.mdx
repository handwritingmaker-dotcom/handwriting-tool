## Tips to Make Handwriting Look More Realistic

If you want your handwritten output to look natural, follow these tips.

### Use Slightly Uneven Spacing

Perfect alignment can look artificial. Slight variations in spacing, size, and line flow help the final output feel more natural.

### Choose Natural Fonts

Avoid overly stylized fonts. Simple handwriting styles usually work best for readable assignments and handwritten notes.

### Adjust Line Spacing

Proper spacing makes your content look more like real handwriting. If the text feels crowded, increase the line spacing before downloading.

### Keep Paragraphs Short

Handwritten content usually looks better with shorter paragraphs. Long blocks of text can feel dense and less natural on a page.

### Use the Right Paper Style

Lined paper is good for assignments and school notes. Blank paper works well for clean documents. Graph paper is useful for lab records, calculations, and structured notes.

### Preview Before Downloading

Always check the preview before exporting. A quick review helps you catch spacing problems, awkward line breaks, or text that needs better formatting.
